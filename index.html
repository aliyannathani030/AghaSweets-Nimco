Our Story

Three generations of the same recipe book

Agha Sweets & Nimco has been part of the neighbourhood since 1990 — starting as a small mithai counter and growing into a one-stop shop for sweets, namkeen, and chilled drinks. What hasn't changed is the recipe book: fresh ghee, no shortcuts, and the same trusted taste every single day.
